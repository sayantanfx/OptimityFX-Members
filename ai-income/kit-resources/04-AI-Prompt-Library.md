# 🧠 AI Prompt Library
### Plug-and-play prompts. Paste into ChatGPT/Claude and fill the [blanks].

---

## ✍️ CONTENT & WRITING

**Instagram captions**
> Act as a social media copywriter. Write 10 Instagram captions for [business type] targeting [audience]. Tone: [friendly/premium]. Each caption: a scroll-stopping first line, value, and a CTA. Add 5 relevant hashtags each.

**Blog/SEO article**
> Write a 1000-word SEO blog post on "[topic]" for [audience]. Include an H1, 4 H2 subheadings, a meta description, and naturally use these keywords: [keywords]. Tone: [tone].

**Resume rewrite**
> You are an expert resume writer. Rewrite the resume below for a [role] position. Use strong action verbs, quantify achievements, optimise for ATS with [industry] keywords, keep to 1 page. Resume: [paste].

**Product description (e-commerce)**
> Write a high-converting product description for [product]. Include a benefit-driven headline, 4 feature→benefit bullets, a short emotional paragraph, and SEO keywords: [keywords]. Tone: [brand voice].

**Email / newsletter**
> Write a [welcome/promotional/newsletter] email for [brand] selling [product] to [audience]. Include a subject line, preview text, body, and one clear CTA. Tone: [tone].

---

## 📱 SOCIAL & MARKETING

**30-day content calendar**
> Create a 30-day Instagram content calendar for [business]. For each day give: content type (reel/carousel/story), a hook, and a caption idea. Mix education, entertainment, and promotion in a 3:1 ratio.

**Ad copy + hooks**
> Write 10 scroll-stopping ad hooks and 3 full ad copies for [product] targeting [audience] on [Meta/Google]. Focus on [pain point]. Avoid income guarantees or unrealistic claims.

**Reel/video script**
> Write a 30-second reel script for [topic] aimed at [audience]. Start with a 3-second hook, deliver 3 quick value points, end with a CTA. Add on-screen text suggestions.

## 🎨 DESIGN & CREATIVE

**Product photo scene (image prompt)**
> A professional studio product photo of [product] on [surface/background], [lighting style] lighting, [mood], high detail, e-commerce ready, 1:1 ratio.

**Logo concept brief**
> Suggest 5 logo concepts for [brand], a [industry] business with a [adjective] personality. For each: style, colour palette, and symbol idea.

**Thumbnail ideas**
> Suggest 5 high-CTR YouTube thumbnail concepts for a video titled "[title]". For each: main text (3-4 words), facial expression, and background idea.

---

## 🤖 AUTOMATION & TECH

**Chatbot FAQ flow**
> Create a customer-support chatbot script for a [business]. List the top 15 FAQs with concise answers, a greeting, a lead-capture step (name + phone), and a fallback to connect to a human.

**Business automation audit**
> I run a [business]. List 10 repetitive manual tasks I could automate with AI/no-code tools (Make/Zapier), the tools to use for each, and the rough time saved per week.

---

## 💼 SALES & CLIENT WORK

**Proposal generator**
> Write a short, persuasive project proposal. Client: [name/business]. Their problem: [problem]. My service: [service]. Deliverables: [list]. Price: ₹[price]. Keep it under 250 words, confident but not pushy.

**Price objection responder**
> A client says my price of ₹[price] for [service] is too high. Give me 3 polite, confident responses that reframe value without immediately discounting.

---

*Pro tip: always edit AI output for facts, tone, and local context. Your judgement is the value clients pay for — AI is just the speed.*
