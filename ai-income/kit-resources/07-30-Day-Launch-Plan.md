# ✅ Your 30-Day Launch Plan

Tick one thing off at a time. Don't skip ahead. This is the difference between "I have ideas" and "I have income."

---

## Week 1 — Set up (Days 1–7)
- [ ] **Day 1:** Pick ONE idea from the 50. Write down why it fits your skills + free hours.
- [ ] **Day 2:** Read that idea's 4-step funnel + deep-dive in the Blueprint.
- [ ] **Day 3–4:** Learn the core AI tool for it. Watch 2–3 tutorials.
- [ ] **Day 5:** Create your **first free sample** using the tool.
- [ ] **Day 6:** Create samples **2 and 3**. These are your portfolio.
- [ ] **Day 7:** Build your portfolio + service menu (use the templates in this pack).

## Week 2 — Reach out (Days 8–14)
- [ ] **Day 8:** Make a list of 30 potential clients (Google Maps, Instagram, LinkedIn).
- [ ] **Day 9–13:** Send **10 outreach messages/day** using the scripts. (That's 50 this week.)
- [ ] **Day 14:** Reply to everyone who responded. Send free samples.

## Week 3 — Close (Days 15–21)
- [ ] **Day 15–17:** Follow up with everyone who didn't reply (use follow-up scripts).
- [ ] **Day 18–19:** Send proposals to interested leads (use the proposal template).
- [ ] **Day 20:** Close your **first client**. Take 50% advance. 🎉
- [ ] **Day 21:** Start the work. Communicate clearly.

## Week 4 — Deliver & grow (Days 22–30)
- [ ] **Day 22–25:** Deliver excellent work. Over-deliver a little.
- [ ] **Day 26:** Collect final payment. Send an invoice.
- [ ] **Day 27:** Ask for a **1-line review** + **2 referrals**.
- [ ] **Day 28:** Update your portfolio with the new work + review.
- [ ] **Day 29:** **Raise your price** for the next client.
- [ ] **Day 30:** Send 10 new outreach messages. Repeat the cycle.

---

## 🎯 The only 3 rules
1. **Consistency > intensity.** 10 messages every day beats 100 once.
2. **Done > perfect.** Your first sample won't be amazing. Send it anyway.
3. **Every client → a review + 2 referrals.** This is how it compounds.

> Most people quit in Week 2 — before a single client says yes. Don't be most people.
