## 🧠 5 Pricing Rules

1. **Charge for the outcome, not the hours.** AI makes you fast — don't price like slow work.
2. **Start slightly low for your first 3 clients** to collect reviews, then raise fast.
3. **Always offer 3 tiers.** Most clients pick the middle one — make it your target price.
4. **Use prices ending in 9** (₹2,999 not ₹3,000) — converts better.
5. **Prefer monthly retainers** over one-off jobs. Recurring income = stability.

---

## 💳 How to get paid (India)
- **UPI** (GPay/PhonePe/Paytm) — fastest, zero friction for small jobs.
- **Razorpay / Cashfree payment links** — professional, supports cards + EMI.
- **Always take 50% advance.** It filters out non-serious clients and protects you.
- Send a proper **invoice** (use `Invoice-Template.xlsx`) — it makes you look established.

---

## ⚠️ Reality check
Undercharging forever is the #1 way freelancers burn out. Your first client is for the review; your tenth is for the income. Raise prices without guilt as your portfolio grows.
