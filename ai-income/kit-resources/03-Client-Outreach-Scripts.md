# 💬 Client Outreach Scripts
### Replace the [bracketed] parts and send. Written to start conversations, not get ignored.

---

## 📩 INSTAGRAM DMs

**1. Cold DM (with free gift) — highest reply rate**
> Hi [Name] 👋 Came across [their business] — love what you're doing with [specific detail]. I noticed your [captions/photos/page] could pull in more customers with a small tweak, so I made you a free sample to show what I mean — no strings 👇 [attach sample]. If you like it, I help businesses like yours with [service]. Want a quick plan?

**2. Cold DM (short version)**
> Hey [Name]! I help [type of business] get more [result] with [service]. Made a quick free sample for you — mind if I send it over?

**3. After they view but don't reply**
> No worries if now's not the right time 🙂 Want me to send that [sample/plan] anyway so you have it?

---

## 📧 COLD EMAILS

**4. Founder/brand email (show, don't tell)**
> Subject: quick idea for [Brand]'s [area]
>
> Hi [Name], I rewrote your [welcome email/product page/ad] — attaching it so you can judge for yourself (took 20 min with my system). If useful, I do this for [type of brands] on a simple monthly plan. Worth a 10-min call this week?
>
> [Your name] · [portfolio link]

**5. Service pitch email**
> Subject: helping [Brand] save time on [task]
>
> Hi [Name], I help [businesses like theirs] with [service] using AI — faster and cheaper than an agency. Here's a sample of my work: [link]. Open to a quick chat?

**6. Email follow-up (3 days later)**
> Hi [Name], floating this back to the top 🙂 Should I send over that [sample/plan], or is now not the right time?

---

## 📱 WHATSAPP

**7. Warm/referral intro**
> Hi [Name]! [Referrer] mentioned you're looking for help with [service]. I've helped [similar client] get [result]. Happy to do a free [audit/sample] first so you can see if it's a fit — shall I?

**8. Local business walk-up follow-up**
> Hi [Name], great meeting you at [place] earlier! As promised, here's a quick sample of [service] for [their business] 👇 Let me know what you think.

**9. Festival/season offer**
> Hi [Name]! [Festival] is coming — a great time to boost [their business]. I'm offering [service] at a special rate till [date]. Want the details?

---

## 🔁 FOLLOW-UPS (50% of deals come from these)

**10.** > Hey [Name], just checking in — still keen on [service]? No pressure either way!

**11.** > Hi [Name], I have 1 slot opening up next week. Want me to hold it for you?

---

## 💵 CLOSING SCRIPTS

**12. State price + timeline + next step in ONE message**
> Great! Here's how it works: [deliverable] for ₹[price]. I take 50% to start and 50% on delivery via UPI. I can begin [day] and deliver by [day]. Shall I send the payment link?

**13. Handling "it's too expensive"**
> Totally understand! I also have a [smaller package] at ₹[lower price] that covers [core deliverable] — want me to start you there?

**14. Handling "let me think about it"**
> Of course! While you decide — would a small paid trial ([1 deliverable] for ₹[small price]) help you see the quality first?

**15. After delivering — ask for review + referral**
> So glad you liked it, [Name]! Two quick favours: (1) could you share a 1-line review I can show others? (2) Know anyone else who needs [service]? I'll give them the same care 🙏

---

*Rule: lead with value (a free sample), keep it short, and always end with a clear next step.*
