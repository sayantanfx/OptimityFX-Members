Module 1

💬 Client Outreach Scripts

Replace the [green] parts and send. These are written to start conversations, not get ignored.

📩 Instagram DM — cold (service offer)
Hi [Name] 👋 I came across [their business] — love what you're doing with [specific detail]. I noticed your [captions/photos/page] could pull in more customers with a small tweak. I actually made you a free sample so you can see what I mean — no strings 👇 [attach 1 sample] If you like it, I help businesses like yours with [service]. Want me to share a quick plan?
Why it works: leads with a free gift, not a pitch. Reply rate jumps 3–4x.
📧 Cold email — to founders/brands
Subject: quick idea for [Brand]'s [area] Hi [Name], I rewrote your [welcome email/product page/ad] — attaching it so you can judge for yourself (took me 20 min with my system). If it's useful, I do this for [type of brands] on a simple monthly plan. Worth a 10-min call this week? [Your name] · [portfolio link]
Keep it under 80 words. Show, don't tell.
📱 WhatsApp — warm/referral follow-up
Hi [Name]! [Referrer] mentioned you're looking for help with [service]. I've helped [similar client] get [result]. Happy to do a free [audit/sample] for you first so you can see if it's a fit — shall I?
Referrals close 5x easier — always ask every happy client for one.
🔁 Follow-up (no reply after 3 days)
Hey [Name], floating this back up 🙂 No pressure at all — should I send over that [sample/plan], or is now not the right time?
~50% of deals come from the follow-up. Always send 1–2.
💵 Closing — turning interest into payment
Great! Here's how it works: [deliverable] for [₹price]. I take 50% to start and 50% on delivery via UPI. I can begin [day] and deliver by [day]. Shall I send the payment link?
Always state price + timeline + next step in ONE message. Reduces drop-off.

Module 5

📈 3 Case Studies to copy

Realistic, honest journeys — including the slow parts. (Illustrative composites, not income guarantees.)

Case 1 — College student → ₹18k/month
Idea: Resume + LinkedIn service · Time: ~10 hrs/week
  1. Made 3 sample resumes, posted a "free teardown" reel.
  2. Messaged 25 final-year students; 6 replied, 3 paid.
  3. Week 4: 8 clients via referrals at ₹499 avg.
  4. Month 3: ₹699 combo + ₹999 premium = ~₹18k/mo.
Key lever: asking every client for a referral. 70% of growth came from word-of-mouth.
Case 2 — Working professional → ₹30k side income
Idea: AI Social Media Manager · Time: weekends + evenings
  1. Audited a local café's page free; landed them at ₹8k/mo.
  2. Batched a month of content in one weekend with AI + Canva.
  3. Used that result to pitch 2 more local brands.
  4. Month 2: 3 retainers = ₹30k while keeping the day job.
Key lever: recurring retainers beat one-off gigs for stable income.
Case 3 — Homemaker → ₹12k passive (growing)
Idea: Digital products (Canva packs) · Time: flexible
  1. Designed 25 Instagram templates for cafés.
  2. Listed on Gumroad at ₹499; posted 3 demo reels/day.
  3. First sale day 11. Slow but steady after.
  4. Month 4: ~₹12k/mo passive + a 2nd pack launched.
Key lever: consistency through the quiet weeks. Most quit before sale #1.

These illustrate realistic paths & effort. Your results depend on your consistency — no guarantees, ever.
