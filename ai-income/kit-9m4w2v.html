📩 Instagram DMs
Cold DM with free gift best reply rate
Hi [Name] 👋 Came across [their business] — love what you're doing with [specific detail]. I noticed your [captions/photos/page] could pull in more customers with a small tweak, so I made you a free sample to show what I mean — no strings 👇 [attach sample]. If you like it, I help businesses like yours with [service]. Want a quick plan?
Leads with a gift, not a pitch — replies jump 3–4x.
Cold DM — short version
Hey [Name]! I help [type of business] get more [result] with [service]. Made a quick free sample for you — mind if I send it over?
📧 Cold Emails
Founder email (show, don't tell)
Subject: quick idea for [Brand]'s [area] Hi [Name], I rewrote your [welcome email/product page/ad] — attaching it so you can judge for yourself (took 20 min with my system). If useful, I do this for [type of brands] on a simple monthly plan. Worth a 10-min call this week? [Your name] · [portfolio link]
Service pitch email
Subject: helping [Brand] save time on [task] Hi [Name], I help [businesses like theirs] with [service] using AI — faster and cheaper than an agency. Here's a sample of my work: [link]. Open to a quick chat?
📱 WhatsApp
Warm / referral intro
Hi [Name]! [Referrer] mentioned you're looking for help with [service]. I've helped [similar client] get [result]. Happy to do a free [audit/sample] first so you can see if it's a fit — shall I?
Festival / season offer
Hi [Name]! [Festival] is coming — a great time to boost [their business]. I'm offering [service] at a special rate till [date]. Want the details?
🔁 Follow-ups (50% of deals come from these)
Gentle follow-up
Hey [Name], floating this back to the top 🙂 Should I send over that [sample/plan], or is now not the right time?
Scarcity follow-up
Hi [Name], I have 1 slot opening up next week. Want me to hold it for you?
💵 Closing
Close (price + timeline + next step)
Great! Here's how it works: [deliverable] for ₹[price]. I take 50% to start and 50% on delivery via UPI. I can begin [day] and deliver by [day]. Shall I send the payment link?
Always state price + timeline + next step in ONE message.
Handle "it's too expensive"
Totally understand! I also have a [smaller package] at ₹[lower price] that covers [core deliverable] — want me to start you there?
After delivery — ask for review + referral
So glad you liked it, [Name]! Two quick favours: (1) could you share a 1-line review I can show others? (2) Know anyone else who needs [service]? I'll give them the same care 🙏

✍️ Content & Writing
Instagram captions
Act as a social media copywriter. Write 10 Instagram captions for [business type] targeting [audience]. Tone: [friendly/premium]. Each caption: a scroll-stopping first line, value, and a CTA. Add 5 relevant hashtags each.
Blog / SEO article
Write a 1000-word SEO blog post on "[topic]" for [audience]. Include an H1, 4 H2 subheadings, a meta description, and naturally use these keywords: [keywords]. Tone: [tone].
Resume rewrite
You are an expert resume writer. Rewrite the resume below for a [role] position. Use strong action verbs, quantify achievements, optimise for ATS with [industry] keywords, keep to 1 page. Resume: [paste].
Product description (e-commerce)
Write a high-converting product description for [product]. Include a benefit-driven headline, 4 feature→benefit bullets, a short emotional paragraph, and SEO keywords: [keywords]. Tone: [brand voice].
📱 Social & Marketing
30-day content calendar
Create a 30-day Instagram content calendar for [business]. For each day give: content type (reel/carousel/story), a hook, and a caption idea. Mix education, entertainment, and promotion in a 3:1 ratio.
Ad copy + hooks
Write 10 scroll-stopping ad hooks and 3 full ad copies for [product] targeting [audience] on [Meta/Google]. Focus on [pain point]. Avoid income guarantees or unrealistic claims.
Reel / video script
Write a 30-second reel script for [topic] aimed at [audience]. Start with a 3-second hook, deliver 3 quick value points, end with a CTA. Add on-screen text suggestions.

🤖 Automation & Tech
Chatbot FAQ flow

Start here, deliver well, raise prices every 3–5 clients.

🧠 5 pricing rules
  1. Charge for the outcome, not the hours.
  2. Start low for your first 3 clients (for reviews), then raise fast.
  3. Always offer 3 tiers — most pick the middle (make it your target).
  4. Use prices ending in 9 (₹2,999 not ₹3,000).
  5. Prefer monthly retainers over one-off jobs.
💳 Always take 50% advance.

It filters non-serious clients and protects you. Send a proper invoice (Templates tab) — it makes you look established.

✅ Your 30-Day Launch Plan

Tick one thing at a time. Don't skip ahead.

Week 1 — Set up
  • Day 1: Pick ONE idea. Write why it fits you.
  • Day 2: Read its funnel + deep-dive in the Blueprint.
  • Day 3–4: Learn the core AI tool.
  • Day 5–6: Make 3 free samples (your portfolio).
  • Day 7: Build portfolio + service menu (Templates tab).
Week 2 — Reach out
  • Day 8: List 30 potential clients.
  • Day 9–13: Send 10 messages/day (Scripts tab).
  • Day 14: Reply to everyone; send free samples.
Week 3 — Close
  • Day 15–17: Follow up with non-repliers.
  • Day 18–19: Send proposals to interested leads.
  • Day 20: Close your first client (50% advance). 🎉
  • Day 21: Start the work.
Week 4 — Deliver & grow
  • Day 22–25: Deliver excellent work.
  • Day 26: Collect final payment; send invoice.
  • Day 27: Ask for review + 2 referrals.
  • Day 28: Update portfolio.
  • Day 29: Raise your price.
  • Day 30: Send 10 new messages. Repeat.
🎯 The only 3 rules

1. Consistency > intensity. 2. Done > perfect. 3. Every client → a review + 2 referrals.
